Privacy Policy

Effective: February 24, 2026

Aydan Automates ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website aydanautomates.com. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site or use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact form:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Business name (optional)
  • Message content

We also automatically collect basic server logs, which may include your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. These are used solely for security and rate-limiting purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Schedule consultations or calls
  • Provide our consulting and coaching services
  • Protect against spam and abuse (rate limiting)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Email Communications

When you submit the contact form, your message is delivered to us via email. We may respond to you at the email address you provide. We will never add you to a mailing list without your explicit consent.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — to serve web fonts. Google may collect basic usage data as described in their privacy policy.
  • Vercel — to host our website. Vercel may collect basic analytics and server logs as described in their privacy policy.
  • Forward Email — to process email delivery from our contact form.

Cookies

Our website does not use cookies or tracking scripts. We do not use analytics services such as Google Analytics.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email inbox for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted to and from our website, and secure SMTP for email delivery.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for us to contact you

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please reach out through our contact form or email us at operator@aydanautomates.com.
